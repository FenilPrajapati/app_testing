Overview

FreightBox is an ocean freight management system, which is a process of efficiently and strategically moving freight across a network from its point of origin to its desired destination using various modes of transportation, intermediaries, and technologies. If you are a freight company and using odoo to manage your processes, Freightbox will be vital for you. It is an end to end process from Booking till release cargo and container return.

DCSA

Our Freight Management is insprired by latest DCSA Standards. Digital Container Shipping Association (DCSA) is an organization established in 2019 by several of the largest container shipping companies. They've established standards for a common technology foundation that enables global collaboration. Their goal is to make shipping services easy to use, flexible, efficient, reliable and environmentally friendly and we ensure that the goal is met.
